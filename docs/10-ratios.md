# 10 Financial Ratios Explained

| Ratio | Formula | What It Tells |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| P/E Ratio | Price / EPS | Valuation |
| ROE | Net Income / Equity | Profitability |
| Debt-to-Equity | Total Debt / Equity | Leverage |
| Current Ratio | CA / CL | Liquidity |
| EPS | Net Income / Shares | Profit per share |
